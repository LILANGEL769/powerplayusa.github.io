Power Play USA


The year is 1960. The world stands on the brink of nuclear warfare. A brutal conflict rages in Vietnam. The country finds itself torn in half over segregation.

American history stand to be shaped by your actions.


Register Login

World's Greatest 20th Century Political Simulation Game

Create Your Political Story

Power Play USA offers players the opportunity to forge their own historical political career. Choose your politician's name, appearance, personal characteristics, and political views. Become a congressman, senator, governor, Supreme Court justice, or reach for the nation's highest office, president.

Participate In American Government

Power Play USA has full functionality for all elected offices. As a governor, shift states towards the positions of your party. As a senator and a representative, pass legislation to change America's political climate. As a justice, decide on the constitutionality of new laws. As a president, sign and veto legislation, and appoint cabinet officials to aid you. Every action has in game consequences, which serve to shape future eras.

Experience Partisan Politics

Power Play USA abandons the traditional two party system, opting instead for a three party system that better represents historic political divisions. Players can join any of the three parties, run for leadership, receive funding in races, and interact with a thriving Discord community. The game also offers the ability to create custom caucuses, furthering cooperation between members of the same party.
